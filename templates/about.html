BestNeighbor

The COVID_19 pandemic has severely impacted the most vulnerable members of our community, and it has strained those working on the frontlines of the response. Our team wanted to provide our community with a tool where people in need can ask for help in getting grocery items, and volunteers can log in and provide the help. A user in need could range from a person whose health makes them vulnerable enough to leave the house, a healthcare worker who’s too busy in the frontlines to get groceries for their family, or a single parent who can’t leave their kids alone. Through BestNeighbor, a person in need of help can provide a wishlist of items they need from a local store along with their zip-code. Volunteers can then log in and see wishlists available within a selected zip-code. These two users will be able to contact each other and coordinate payment and delivery.
